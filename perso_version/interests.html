INTERESTS

Piano

I have practiced piano for 10 years.
I really love it as it allows me to relax while stimulating my artistic side. I've also always loved working deeply the pieces I played : I really don't like switching to another if it's not perfectly played!

Sound mixing

I discovered sound mixing when I was in secondary school. It instantly captivated me. It all consists in making a bunch of tracks sound pleasurable to the listener of your music or film. We could say it's kind of magical.
When I entered ENSEEIHT, I discovered the audiovisual CAn7 association. Its job is to manage the lights and sound at every ENSEEIHT event, or during services for districts, congresses, or even other schools. It's now my new family!

Photo

I got my first reflex camera at 12. I took many photos during my trips or holidays.
When I entered my preparatory class, nobody was in charge of taking photos during the events there was. So I've really a few memories of my first year.
Therefore, I decided to take the role of photographer during my second year. It was the best decision I made. You make everybody happy (including you) in creating physical memories and they return the sentiment. That's really honoring.
Thus, I decided to repeat the experience at ENSEEIHT. But I like a bit less because there are so much more people (as people getting photografied, or as photographers).

Cycling

Cycling is my favourite sport. I love doing sport while visiting new places and discovering new landcapes.
The best activity in cycling is going to mountain. It's painful, but so beautiful!
