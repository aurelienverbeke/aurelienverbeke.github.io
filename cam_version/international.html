INTERNATIONAL MOBILITY

Barcelona (2024)

Between my preparatory class and engineering school, I went in Barcelona with friends during one week.
It was both a way to recharge our batteries and discover new cultures, new people and new landscapes. That was quite tiring but such a wonderful experience.
We visited the city, its architectural monuments, its streets, but also, obvisouly, its beaches.

Trip in Europe (2019)

At 14, I made a two-weeks summer camp in Europe. We crossed four cities in four countries : Berlin in Germany,
Vienna in Austria, Prague in Czech Republic, and Venice in Italia.
Switching from one culture to another was quite disconcerting but also really enriching.
