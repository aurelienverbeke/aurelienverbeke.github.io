STUDIES

ENSEEIHT (2024 -)

ENSEEIHT is a famous engineering school in IT, electronics and hydraulics from the INP (National Polytechnic Institute) group.
Located in Toulouse, France, it's in an innovative and dynamic cluster supported by the aeronautic industry.
As I wanted to study IT, I chose the ENSEEIHT for the diversity of its courses. It allows us to focus on a specific domain while having a deep view of the IT world.



At ENSEEIHT, I'm involved in 4 associations and clubs : CAn7 (audiovisual, sound and light), Photo7, net7 (student IT networks management) and Classic7 (orchestra playing modern classical music).
I also take part in many councils as a student representative.

La Prépa des INP
(2022 - 2024)

La Prépa des INP, formerly known as CPP (Polytechnic Preparatory Cycle), is an integrated preparatory class to the INP group.
It gives the students all the technical knowledge and soft skills they need to enter an engineering school.
Though it's a general scientific training, I specialized in mathematics and IT, with the aim of entering a IT engineering school.



In second year, I took the role of photographer for the student association.
I was also a student representative for my group in the teachers' conference, and for the entire school year at the board of directors.
