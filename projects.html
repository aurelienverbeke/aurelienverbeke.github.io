Météo-France internship

At the end of my preparatory class, I made a 3-month internship in Météo-France.
Météo-France is the french national weather service. It collects a lot of data coming from the radars, ground stations, radiosondes and ocean stations and both predicts the weather and analyses climatology.
Accordingly, I made a comparison tool for weathers radars. Indeed, Météo-France needs to ensure the quality of its mesures, when installing a new radar or for a daily monitoring.

Visu-MF

Visu-MF is a personal project. It's a website which displays some data produced by Météo-France.
For now, you can see radar mosaics and ground observations (temperature, pressure, rain quantity).
In the future, we can also visualize individual radar images or forecast models outputs.
